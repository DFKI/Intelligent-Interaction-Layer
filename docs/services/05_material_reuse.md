# Material Reuse Potential

Assesses how reusable or recyclable a material stream is — giving a reuse score,
the best recovery pathways, an estimated market value per kg, and the CO₂ saved
versus making the material from scratch.

## What you can ask

> Assess the reuse potential of 200 kg of aluminum in good condition with low contamination.
>
> What are the recycling pathways for PET in fair condition?
>
> How much CO₂ can I save by recycling 1 tonne of steel?

## What you get back

- A reuse potential (High / Medium / Low) and score
- Ranked recovery pathways with how much value each recovers
- The processing steps needed
- An estimated market value per kg and CO₂ saved vs. virgin material

## Good to know

- Just say the material, its rough condition, and (optionally) how much you have.
- Supported materials include aluminum, copper, steel, PET, HDPE, glass, paper,
  and e-waste; anything else uses a generic estimate.
