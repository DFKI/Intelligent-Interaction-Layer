# Service Registry

Lists the Circular Economy services available and what each one does — a handy
first question when you're not sure where to start.

## What you can ask

> What CE services are available?
>
> List everything you can help me with.
>
> What can this assistant do?

## What you get back

A directory of the 5 services, each with a short description, the information it
needs, and the standards it relates to.
