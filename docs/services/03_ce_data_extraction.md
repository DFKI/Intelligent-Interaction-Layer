# CE Data Extraction

Reads a product description, datasheet, or sustainability report and pulls out the
circular-economy facts — materials, recycled content, recyclability,
certifications, hazardous substances, and end-of-life options.

## What you can ask

> Extract the CE data from this text: "Made from 30% recycled steel, REACH compliant, 75% recyclable, take-back scheme available."
>
> What circular-economy information is in this datasheet? [paste the text]
>
> Pull the recycled content and certifications from this description.

## What you get back

- The materials found
- Recycled content and recyclability percentages (when stated)
- Certifications, hazardous substances, and end-of-life options
- A note on which CE details were missing

## Good to know

- You can paste the extracted details straight into a follow-up question —
  e.g. "now give me its circularity score" — and the assistant carries them over.
